About Kencom Softwares Ltd

East Africa's Premier ERP & Enterprise Solutions Provider with 14+ Years of Excellence

Kencom Softwares Ltd stands as East Africa's most trusted technology partner, pioneering digital transformation across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda since 2010. We are the region's leading specialist in comprehensive ERP solutions, custom enterprise applications, and mission-critical business systems that power organizations from SMEs to Fortune 500 companies.

Our expertise spans diverse industries including healthcare (managing 500,000+ patient records), higher education (serving 100,000+ students across multiple universities), manufacturing (optimizing complex supply chains), financial services, and government institutions. We've successfully deployed 180+ enterprise systems, transforming how businesses operate and compete in the digital economy.

As Microsoft Gold Partners, SAP Technology Partners, and Oracle Solutions Providers, we leverage cutting-edge technologies including AI, machine learning, cloud computing, and blockchain to create solutions that don't just meet today's needs – they anticipate tomorrow's challenges. Our commitment to innovation has earned us 15+ industry awards and the trust of East Africa's most demanding organizations.
